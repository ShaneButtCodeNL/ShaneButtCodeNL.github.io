About Me

I am from a small town in Newfoundland called Holyrood. Growing up I had an interest in computers steming from playing around with my old commodore 64, and a love of video games. Some of my other interests include Maths, logic, table top games, music, and card games.

I spend most of my free time between learning new technologies and my sax. Some tech I've been learning recently are React framework, CSS frameworks like SASS, AWS, and SVG creation. Some things that I will be learning next are React Native.
